Session issue

Your study session may have timed out, or your browser may be blocking storage (cookies/local data) needed to run the task.

Most common causes

  • Opened an incorrect link (or used an old bookmark).
  • Refreshed the page during the task.
  • Private browsing mode or strict privacy settings blocked site storage.
  • Your network/IP changed during the session (some networks do this).

How to fix it

  1. Close this tab.
  2. Open the study from the main entry page.
  3. If it happens again, enable cookies / site storage for this domain and try again.
