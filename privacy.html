Privacy and data handling

This site is designed to collect anonymous task responses and brief self-report items.

Once configured, data transmission will occur over HTTPS to a secure endpoint and stored in restricted-access storage.

What we collect

  • Reaction-time and accuracy data from task trials
  • Brief self-report responses (non-identifying)
  • Basic device metadata (browser type, screen size) for quality control

What we do not collect

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Direct identifiers
  • Precise location
